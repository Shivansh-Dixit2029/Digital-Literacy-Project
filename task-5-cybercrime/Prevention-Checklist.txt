Stay Safe Online – Checklist

1. Never share your OTP, PIN, passwords, or bank details with anyone, even if they claim to be from a bank or official source.

2. Always verify the sender’s email address or phone number before clicking on any link or downloading attachments.

3. Do not click on unknown or suspicious links, especially those received through SMS, WhatsApp, or email.

4. Use strong and unique passwords for different accounts and enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible.

5. Avoid using public Wi-Fi for logging into sensitive accounts like banking or email.

6. For UPI safety: Never approve payment requests unless you are sure about the sender. Remember, receiving money does not require entering your PIN.

7. For financial safety: Do not scan random QR codes or accept “collect requests” from unknown users, as they can lead to money being deducted.

8. Regularly update your apps and devices to protect against security vulnerabilities.

9. Be cautious while sharing personal information on social media platforms.

10. In case of any cyber fraud or suspicious activity, immediately report it on the National Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in) or call the helpline number 1930.
