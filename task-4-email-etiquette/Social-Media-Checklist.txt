Social Media Do’s and Don’ts

DO’s:
1. Use a professional and appropriate profile picture.
2. Think carefully before posting any content online.
3. Keep your privacy settings properly configured.
4. Share useful, informative, or positive content.
5. Respect others’ opinions and communicate politely.

DON’Ts:
1. Don’t share personal or sensitive information publicly.
2. Don’t use abusive or offensive language.
3. Don’t spread fake news or unverified information.
4. Don’t post inappropriate or harmful content.
5. Don’t engage in unnecessary online arguments or conflicts.
